Ayurvedic Approach to Panchakarma Treatment

Collection of various impurities inside human body causes various diseases, is one of the acclaimed doctrines of Ayurveda. When not properly eliminated, impurities like feces collect in the body and when body fails to assimilate them,they lead to different kinds of diseases. Panchakarma treatment in Tuticorin is a set of treatments to eradicate impurities and keep the body clean.

Purification Therapies of Panchakarma Treatment Consists of:

Pre Purification Procedure

This is the first phase of Panchakarma treatment in Tuticorin. The Pre Purification Therapy includes internal oleation (snehapanam - oral administration of medicated ghee) and external oleation (Swedana - medicated steam bath). . This may last 3 to 5 days.

Main Purification procedure

This is the second and main phase of Panchakarma treatment in Tuticorin. This treatment last 3 to 5 days. The main purification includes (Vamana, Virechana, Vasti, Nasya and Rakhtamokshana) this is main stage of panchakarma treatment where mobilized toxins are expelled through the natural channels of the body. Strict diet will be followed by the whole treatment as advised by our doctor.

Post Purification procedure

This is the last and final phase of panchakarma treatment in Tuticorin . Post Purification procedure includes rejuvenation treatments and herbal formulas, diet and lifestyle guidelines to help you maximize the benefits of the panchakarma and build a strong foundation for good health. The rejuvenation treatments and internal medicines are given to the patient in rest of the days. All the medicines are provided with in the resort and diet will prepared with in the kitchen as per the doctors advice.
