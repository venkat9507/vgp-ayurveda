Ayurvedic Treatment for Indigestion

In VGP Dhanvanthari Ayurvedic Resort offers unique ayurvedic treatment for indigestion problems. Indigestion when left untreated may result in chronic diseases, especially for those who suffer from them frequently. Digestive disorders could be a result of poor digestion which may occur due to symptoms like excessive gas, constipation, diarrhea, burping, burning sensation in chest, vomiting, indigestion etc.

Ayurveda teaches us that digestion is the single most important thing in any human being's life. Ayurveda says " You are what you digest". When you have proper digestion it will reflect in your life, if you have poor digestion, your muscles, blood vessels everything becomes weak and susceptible to disease.

Cause of Indigestion in Ayurveda

In Ayurveda cause of indigestion is pitta dosha. Due to this pitta dosha, there is production of ama (toxins) in the body which causes various stomach compilation like nausea, stomach ulcers, gallstones etc. Ayurvedic treatment for indigestion includes treatments that helps in eliminating toxins from the body.
