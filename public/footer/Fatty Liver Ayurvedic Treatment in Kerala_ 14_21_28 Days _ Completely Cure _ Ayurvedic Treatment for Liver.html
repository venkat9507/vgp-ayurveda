Therefore, the health of the liver required balance in the pitta dosha to support the liver's ability to flush toxins from the body. All the treatments included in fatty liver ayurvedic treatment in Tuticorin will be prescribed after the consultation with our highly qualified Ayurvedic practitioners and also after a complete physical examination.
